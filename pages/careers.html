MAKE STAR MOVES

No matter what store you’re into (we have 7) or what role (we have a lot of those too), Black Star Pastry is the perfect place to hone your craft or kick off a hospitality career.

Joining Black Star Pastry also means you get to access a variety of company benefits like:

  • Birthday Leave - We love celebrations, especially birthday celebrations. One additional annual leave day per year to be taken within a week of your birthday.
  • Taking Care - We know that wellness is both physical and mental, so we have a Take Care day when you need them. Because being unwell is not a day at the beach.
  • Birthday cake - One complimentary cake of your choice to be taken within a week of your birthday.
  • Employee Assistance Program - Free, confidential counselling, support and advice for Black Star Pastry employees through Connect Psych.

From store managers and supervisors, to baristas, chefs and bakers, we have just the job for you.

If you don't think you fit our advertised roles, reach out anyway! We're always on the look out for talented people.
